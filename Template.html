About SCI Committee

The School of Computing & Informatics Committee is the backbone of student engagement and innovation at Al Bukhary International University.

We are dedicated to creating meaningful experiences through technology, fostering intellectual growth, and building connections that last beyond the classroom.

Our Mission: Empowering students to become innovators, leaders, and changemakers in the technology industry.

Through collaborative initiatives, workshops, and events, we bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world application.
