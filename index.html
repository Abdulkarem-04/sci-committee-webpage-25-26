About Us

Who We Are

The SCI Committee is the backbone of the School of Computing & Informatics student community. We represent and advocate for our students, organizing events, initiatives, and fostering innovation across all computing disciplines.

Our Mission

To create opportunities for students to grow academically, professionally, and personally through collaborative initiatives, knowledge sharing, and community engagement in the field of computing and technology.

Our Vision

To be a leading student committee that champions innovation, excellence, and inclusivity, preparing the next generation of computing professionals to make a global impact.
